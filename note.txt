Elevate your match predictions with our AI Football Predictor. 
Using advanced machine learning, it analyzes historical data and player 
stats for accurate forecasts. Customizable and community-driven, stay ahead 
in predicting football outcomes with this powerful tool.


Features:
    Recent Team Performance:
        Goals scored and conceded in the last few matches.
        Possession percentages, shots on target, or other team statistics from recent games.
    Player Form:
        Recent performance metrics for key players.
        Information on player injuries or suspensions from the last few matches.
    Match-Specific Factors:
        Home or away advantage in recent games.
        Weather conditions and their impact on recent matches.
    Head-to-Head Performance:
        Outcomes of the last few matches between the two teams.
    Derived Features:
        Team form based on the results of the last 10 matches.
        Average goals scored or conceded over the last 10 matches.
