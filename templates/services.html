Head-to-Head

This section displays the matches played between the two selected teams, accompanied by a pie chart showing the outcome probabilities of their last encounter. Analyzing head-to-head performance provides valuable insights into their competitive dynamics, helping to predict future match outcomes and understand each team's strengths and weaknesses against the other.

last five match

This section displays the last five matches of the inputted teams using a line chart with three lines (win, draw, loss) showing the probability of each outcome. Additionally, a pie chart highlights the result distribution of their most recent match, providing a clear visual of their current form and performance trends.

home advantage

This section focuses on the selected home team's performance. A line chart displays the outcomes (win, draw, loss) of their home matches, illustrating the probability of each result. This visual analysis highlights the team's strength when playing on their home ground, offering insights into their home advantage.

similar opponent

This section compares the common opponents faced by both user-inputted teams. It includes a pie chart displaying the probability data of match outcomes for each team against these opponents, highlighting wins, losses, and draws. This visual analysis helps to understand each team's performance against shared competitors.
